About Us

Lokacart Plus application is an initiative by IIT Bombay to bridge the gap between consumers and producers in India.It is an android based e-commerce market platform for the Farmer Producer Companies who are involved in direct marketing of products to their customers.It provides economic empowerment to the farmers organizations through mobile applications. Lokacart Plus automates the process of receiving orders,bill generation and delivery processing through mobile application.It is a B2B model that suits the need of the FPCs.In Lokacart Plus buyers can make their requests and order the required products.
